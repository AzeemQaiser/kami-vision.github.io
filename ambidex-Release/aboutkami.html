ABOUT KAMI

The Data Intelligence

company

  • Kami Vision is on a path to changing the world by making video streams from devices extremely useful for consumers and businesses
  • We started off as a consumer software platform business unit of Yi Technologies, a much larger video device manufacturer, in 2017, and have now become an Independent business unit
  • We provide software ecosystems to build vision AI applications for consumers and businesses and hardware design services encompassing vision devices and EDGE IOT equipment

Our mission is to make the world a smarter, safer place to live in with accessible and economical vision AI.
